What is FOT?

"Fields of Tomorrow" (FOT) is a campaign dedicated to supporting farmers through a collaborative partnership with the government.

The primary focus of FOT is to make farming financially stable and sustainable, ensuring that farmers have the resources and knowledge they need to thrive. The campaign has already achieved significant milestones, such as providing sufficient electricity to farmers, which has been instrumental in reducing costs and improving productivity


FOTs core mission revolves around educating, empowering, and supporting farmers in adopting modern technologies and sustainable practices.

By promoting advanced farming techniques, FOT helps farmers enhance their productivity, increase their profits, and contribute positively to environmental stewardship.


The overarching goal of the campaign is to create a brighter and more resilient future for agriculture, ensuring long-term viability and sustainability for the farming community.

Our Goal

The goal of the "Fields of Tomorrow" campaign is to partner with the government to promote sustainable farming, improve farmers' financial stability, and ensure agricultural resilience. It aims to educate, empower, and support farmers in adopting modern technologies and practices to boost productivity, profitability, and environmental sustainability.
